The **Tufts Public Opinion Lab (TPOL)** is dedicated to studying contemporary
controversies in American public opinion using quantitative data analysis.
Lab members publish their research on the
[Lab's blog](https://medium.com/tufts-public-opinion-lab), which attracts
thousands of readers, and in some of the discipline's top journals.

## Joining the Lab

If you are a Tufts student interested in joining the lab,
[click here for more information](https://medium.com/tufts-public-opinion-lab/joining-the-lab).
To be eligible to join, students must have completed PS 103 (Political Science
Research Methods).
